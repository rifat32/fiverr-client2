    On-chain computational agents (Cnidaria), Deeplink Cluster, and On-chain Neural Networks (ONN) enable greater programmability, automation, and analytics for smart contracts. The off-chain data & computation will be provided by Network Validators & off-chain Computation Providers. All on-chain and off-chain communication will go through the Deeplink L2 network & bridge to ensure security, and trustless, scalable operations. The deeplink developer toolkit will allow developers and data scientists to build, train and deploy data models, algorithms, and ML models for smart contracts.
